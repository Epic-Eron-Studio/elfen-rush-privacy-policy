Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Elfen Rush ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our Android game application.

1. Information We Collect

Elfen Rush does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). However, we may collect the following non-personal data:

  • Gameplay statistics (e.g., score, level progress)
  • Device information (e.g., Android version, device model)
  • Crash reports (used to improve app stability)

2. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your non-personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Legitimate Interest: To improve app performance, stability, and user experience.
  • Consent: When required, for services such as analytics or third-party features.

3. Use of Data

Data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Improving game performance and stability
  • Providing leaderboard features via Google Play Games
  • Analyzing aggregate gameplay behavior to enhance future updates

4. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services – for leaderboards and achievements
  • Firebase – for crash reporting and performance monitoring

These services may collect device data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

Elfen Rush does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Data Retention

We retain non-personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are a resident of the EEA, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct or delete your data
  • Right to object to or restrict data processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, contact us at epiceronstudio+support@gmail.com.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

9. Changes to This Policy
